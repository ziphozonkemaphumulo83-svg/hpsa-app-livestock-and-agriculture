Training Manual

Welcome to the HPSA App training manual. This guide helps you understand how to use the app effectively.

1. Logging In

Select the desired report card from the home screen. Project codes are handled internally by the app for authorized access.

2. Offline Mode

The app works offline. Your entries are stored locally and synced automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

3. Navigation

Use the home page cards to access reports and resources. Click Training Manual anytime for help.

4. Support

If you encounter any issues, contact the HPSA support team for assistance.
